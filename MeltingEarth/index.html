The earth is melting

As the green house effect warms up earth, the water contained as ice in both polar ice caps are turning into water, raising the sea level.

This is only one side of the danger that lies a head.

Graph show positive correlation between CO2 emmision level in tones (ourworlddata, annual global CO2 emmision) and changes in sea level in millimeters (NASA, GSML)
